Ai Ping Pong Game













Instructions

1-Keep your laptop screen straight.

2-Move yourself 3-4 feet away from your device

3-Move your right wrist in an ascending and descending manner. A red dot should appear on it

4-Now press the play button and move yourself 3-4 feet away from your device.

5-The red paddle is your paddle and it should move according to your right wrist.
